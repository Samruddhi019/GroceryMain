Proposed Methodology for Online Book Ordering System:

1. **Discussion**: Initiate discussions to define the scope and objectives of the online book ordering system.

2. **Leadership**: Appoint a capable leader and assign roles and responsibilities to team members.

3. **Data Collection**: Gather book information from various sources, including titles, authors, prices, and images.

4. **Data Analysis**: Analyze collected data to ensure accuracy and relevance for the system.

5. **Data Representation**: Present book details in user-friendly formats, including images, titles, and prices.

6. **Report Preparation**: Create a comprehensive project report summarizing system design and functionality.

7. **Task Completion**: Implement the system and submit it for evaluation, ensuring it meets the defined objectives.
